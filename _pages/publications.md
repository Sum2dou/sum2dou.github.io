**Better food system& better world**   Article from American Mathematical Modeling Competition for College Students
[View paper here](../assets/2100419.pdf)<br>
I meticulously developed a multi-factor food system evaluation model, using detailed data to conduct an in-depth analysis of the development trends of the food systems in China and the United States, and designed targeted improvement plans accordingly. To gain a more comprehensive and in-depth understanding of the food system, I introduced the "Food - Energy - Water - Economy - Society" PSR model, revealing the interrelationships and dynamic change laws among various elements from the perspective of system dynamics.I was also responsible for the visualization of various pictures in the paper.
